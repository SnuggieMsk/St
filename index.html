Key Sentiment Concerns

While overall sentiment is positive, analysis revealed several areas for improvement:

  • Pricing of premium items - Ultra Light Down jackets received some price sensitivity feedback
  • Store crowding - Negative sentiment about weekend congestion in Delhi-NCR stores
  • Limited size availability - Some customers reported difficulty finding extended sizes in-store
  • Product availability - Concerns about seasonal items being out of stock quickly

Store Layout

Uniqlo's stores follow a minimalist, functional design with clear product segmentation:

  • Men's, women's, and kids' sections clearly delineated
  • Floor-to-ceiling shelves with folded clothing sorted by color
  • Feature tables near entrances for seasonal collections
  • Digital displays showcasing LifeWear concept
  • Self-service oriented layout with centralized fitting rooms

Merchandise Mix

Product assortment is tailored to market needs:

  • Core basics maintain consistent shelf space year-round
  • Technical products (HEATTECH, AIRism) receive feature positioning
  • Delhi stores stock deeper winter assortment than Mumbai
  • UT graphic tees highlighted for younger demographic
  • Extended sizing introduced for Indian body types

Staff Operations

Each Delhi-NCR store employs 40-45 staff members:

  • Comprehensive training program for Japanese customer service
  • Specialized product knowledge for fabric technology
  • Additional temporary staff during peak seasons (Diwali, end-of-year)
  • Career progression opportunities within company
  • Performance incentives and regular team-building activities
